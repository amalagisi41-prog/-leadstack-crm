# Pipeline stages

Your stages should mirror how a real buyer or seller moves, not how a software company thinks a pipeline should look.

## A simple real estate flow

1. New inquiry
2. Contacted
3. Qualified
4. Showing scheduled
5. Offer in
6. Under contract
7. Closed

## Warning signs

- Too many stages
- Stages that sound similar
- No clear rule for when a deal moves

If a teammate cannot tell the difference between two stages in five seconds, combine them.
