# Booking pages

Booking pages are the cleanest way to turn interest into confirmed appointments. Instead of texting back and forth, a buyer or seller chooses from your available time slots.

## When to use them

- Buyer consultations
- Listing appointments
- Open house follow-up calls
- Quick strategy calls for warm online leads

## What makes them work

- Clear page title
- Short intro explaining what the meeting is for
- Good availability windows
- Confirmation and reminder messages turned on

## Best practice

Create separate booking pages for different appointment types. A listing consult and a buyer intro call should not feel identical to the person booking them.
