# Lead routing

Lead routing decides who owns the next inbound opportunity. If that rule is vague, hot leads sit too long or end up with the wrong person.

## The main routing choices

- Round robin
- Territory based
- Source based
- Always to one primary operator

## What to think through

- Who should get Zillow leads?
- Who should get sphere or referral leads?
- What happens after hours?
- When should the AI escalate to a human immediately?

## Keep it boring

The best routing rule is the one your team can explain in one sentence. If it takes a meeting to understand, it is probably too complicated.
