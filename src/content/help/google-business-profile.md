# Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is one of the simplest local visibility tools you have. It helps with branded search, map visibility, and review trust.

## What to keep current

- Business name
- Phone number
- Hours
- Service area
- Website link
- Recent photos

## Ongoing habits that help

- Post updates regularly
- Respond to reviews
- Keep Q&A clean and current

If your profile is stale, your local presence looks stale too. Small updates matter more than perfect updates.
