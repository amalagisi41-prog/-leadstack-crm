# Connect IDX Broker

AgentStack uses your own IDX Broker account so your listing search stays branded to you and every inquiry lands directly in your CRM.

## The three-step flow

1. Add your IDX Broker credentials.
2. Run a sync test.
3. Go live when the sample listings look right.

## Before you begin

- Make sure your IDX Broker account is active.
- Keep your API key handy.
- Confirm the market area and account you want to connect.

## What to check in the sync test

- Listing photos load
- Prices and addresses look right
- Status labels make sense
- Inquiry forms create leads in the right workspace

## If you want to skip setup

You can still sell IDX as an add-on and have your team finish the connection for the client. The product supports both self-serve and done-for-you delivery.
