# Lead forms

The goal of a lead form is not to ask everything. It is to get enough signal to start the right follow-up.

## Usually enough to start

- Name
- Email or phone
- One intent question

## Helpful extras

- Price range
- Timeline
- Property type
- Whether they already have an agent

## Keep the balance right

Long forms feel precise, but short forms usually convert better. Start short, then let the follow-up sequence gather the rest.
