# Billing and plan changes

Your billing setup should match how you sell and how many people use the workspace. AgentStack supports self-serve plan changes so you do not have to email support for basic account moves.

## Typical plan change moments

- You move from solo production to a small team
- You want brokerage-level visibility
- You add more services and need a larger operating footprint

## What changes immediately

- The plan record on your account
- Any plan-based feature gates
- Future billing at the new amount

## What to double-check after a change

- Team seat access
- Add-on settings
- Billing email and card details

If you are making a bigger operational jump, it is still smart to review your setup before inviting more users into the workspace.
