# Cancel your plan

If you need to cancel, export what matters first and make sure your active conversations are covered somewhere else.

## Before you cancel

- Export contacts
- Export deals or pipeline data you want to keep
- Check any live automations or active messaging flows
- Confirm whether a teammate still needs access

## What to expect

Cancellation stops future billing. Your access and retention window depend on the account state and the current billing period.

## Best practice

Do the export before you click cancel, not after. That keeps the handoff simple and avoids last-minute scrambling.
