# Review requests

Review requests work best when they feel timely and easy. AgentStack is designed to ask right after the moment where trust is highest, not weeks later when the experience is already cold.

## Best timing

- Right after closing
- Right after a successful service handoff
- Right after the client says yes to the experience

## What to avoid

- Asking too early
- Asking everyone the same way
- Sending multiple reminders to the same client too quickly

## Better approach

Use a simple thank-you message, one clear ask, and one clear destination like Google or Zillow.
