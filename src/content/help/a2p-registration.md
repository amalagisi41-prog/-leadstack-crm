# A2P registration

A2P 10DLC is the carrier approval process for business texting in the United States. If you want AgentStack to send real SMS follow-up from your business number, this step matters.

## What you need first

- Your business legal name
- EIN or business tax information
- Website or landing page
- A public privacy policy and terms page
- The exact opt-in language you use on forms

## What AgentStack helps with

AgentStack guides you through the registration flow, keeps the status visible, and stores the compliance details you already need for your lead forms and SMS consent.

## Common delays

- Missing business information
- Weak or unclear opt-in wording
- A website that does not clearly show the business identity

## Good rule of thumb

If a carrier reviewer can quickly see who you are, what texts you send, and how someone opts out, approval usually goes more smoothly.
